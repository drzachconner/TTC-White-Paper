**CRITICAL Fix 1 -- "VSC CANNOT exist without AMT" (Logical Coherence Finding 3 / Top Vulnerability 1)**
Find: The sentence containing "VSC CANNOT exist without AMT" (likely in all-caps emphasis).
Replace with: "TTC hypothesizes that persistent Adverse Mechanical Tension commonly accompanies clinically significant vertebral compensation and may contribute to its maintenance." Do NOT use the reviewer's exact wording if a more voice-appropriate version achieves the same epistemic softening.

**CRITICAL Fix 2 -- Tonal intervention therapeutic exclusivity (Logical Coherence Finding 1)**
Find: "A tonal approach says: if interference is tonal, the adjustment must be tonal." (or similar wording)
Reframe as hypothesis: The idea should read as "if interference is substantially mediated through altered tone, a tonal intervention may offer a more direct engagement than purely osseous methods." Preserve the rhetorical power while removing the false logical necessity.

**CRITICAL Fix 3 -- Leg balancing circular reasoning (Logical Coherence Finding 4 / Evidentiary Finding 6 / Top Vulnerability 3)**
Find ALL passages where leg balancing is described as "verification," "confirmation," or the body "saying yes."
For each instance: Reframe as "internal clinical indicator" or "working clinical heuristic." Add ONE acknowledgment (not at every instance) that independent validation of this indicator remains needed. Do not remove the concept -- just downgrade the epistemic status from "proof" to "operational signal within TTC practice."

**CRITICAL Fix 4 -- Dorrier pathology-to-physiology extrapolation (Evidentiary Finding 1 / Top Vulnerability 2)**
Find: The passage about meningeal myofibroblast activity citing Dorrier 2021, especially where it says "This mechanism has been directly observed in meningeal tissue" and the extension to "chronic subclinical stress."
Reframe: Make explicit that Dorrier demonstrates fibrotic/contractile potential in pathological contexts, and TTC hypothesizes that lower-grade analogues may contribute to persistent tension states -- but this specific extension has not been directly demonstrated. The word "directly observed" must NOT be used for the subclinical extension.

**CRITICAL Fix 5 -- Haavik as "direct validation" (Evidentiary Finding 2 / Top Vulnerability 4)**
Find: "Dr. Haavik's work provided direct scientific validation for tension-based models of subluxation and the neurophysiologic effects of chiropractic care." (or similar)
Replace "direct scientific validation" with language like "compelling evidence that spinal manipulation produces measurable neurophysiologic effects" or "supportive evidence." Make clear Haavik studied articular HVLA manipulation, not TTC's non-articular method. This hedge was partially done in Phase 3 -- check current wording before editing. If Phase 3 already softened this adequately, verify and move on.

**CRITICAL Fix 6 -- Metaphysics contaminating mechanism (Audience Calibration Finding 3 / Top Vulnerability 5)**
Find: The passages where "Universal Intelligence," "the body's 'yes' response," and "congruent intent as signal amplifier" appear in close proximity to mechanistic claims.
Fix: Add a methodological boundary statement early in the paper (ideally at the end of the Executive Summary or start of Part I) along these lines: "This paper includes a philosophical interpretive framework rooted in chiropractic's vitalistic tradition. The clinical model presented, however, is designed to be evaluated on its own terms: anatomical plausibility, internal consistency, and testability." This is a ONE-TIME framing statement, not a per-section disclaimer. Do NOT move all vitalistic language to an appendix -- the white paper audience expects it. The boundary statement simply signals self-awareness.

**CRITICAL Fix 7 -- "Pierces through compensatory layers" (Rhetorical Finding 3)**
Find: "The precisely vectored input... pierces through the compensatory layers to communicate directly with the intelligence of the NeuroSpinal System."
Rewrite to preserve the clinical idea without pseudoscientific-sounding imagery. Something like: "TTC hypothesizes that precise, low-force input may preferentially engage the most clinically relevant layer of a broader compensatory pattern." Keep TTC voice but remove "pierces through" and "intelligence of the NeuroSpinal System" from the same sentence.

**CRITICAL Fix 8 -- Absolute claims + marketing language (Vulnerability Assessment 5)**
Find: "never-ending optimization" and any remaining absolute claims flagged as marketing language.
Replace "never-ending optimization" with "ongoing improvement in function and adaptability" or similar clinical language. Grep for other marketing-adjacent phrases and soften.
